Welcome to Z8! This section will help you get up and running quickly.

## What is Z8?

Z8 is a comprehensive team management system that helps organizations manage:

- Employee profiles and assignments
- Time tracking and work schedules (with GoBD compliance)
- Vacation and absence management
- Team structure and permissions
- Holiday calendars with country presets
- Multi-channel notifications (email, push, in-app)
- Analytics and reporting with export capabilities

## Quick Start

### For Employees

1. **Log in** to your organization's Z8 instance
2. **Complete onboarding** - The guided onboarding flow will walk you through setup
3. **Set up your profile** - Add your name, photo, and notification preferences
4. **Enable notifications** - Allow push notifications for real-time updates
5. **Check your schedule** - View your work hours and vacation balance
6. **Start tracking time** - Use the quick clock in/out widget in the header

### For Managers

1. Complete the employee steps above
2. **Review your team** - See who reports to you
3. **Handle approvals** - Process time-off and correction requests
4. **Monitor schedules** - Ensure adequate team coverage

### For Administrators

1. Complete the manager steps above
2. **Configure your organization** - Set up vacation policies and schedules
3. **Add employees** - Create profiles and assign managers
4. **Manage permissions** - Grant team-level access as needed

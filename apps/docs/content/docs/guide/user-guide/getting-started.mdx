## First Time Login

When you first log in, you'll see:
- Your personal dashboard with relevant widgets
- Navigation menu to access different features
- Your profile information in the top-right corner

---

## Customizing Your Dashboard

Your dashboard displays widgets that provide quick access to important information. You can personalize the layout by rearranging widgets:

### Reordering Widgets

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Click and hold** any widget card on your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Drag** the widget to your preferred position
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Release** to drop the widget in its new location
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your widget arrangement is automatically saved and will persist across sessions.

### Available Widgets

Depending on your role, you may see these widgets:
- **Managed Employees** - Quick access to your team (managers)
- **Pending Approvals** - Requests awaiting your review (managers)
- **Team Overview** - Team status and activity
- **Quick Stats** - Your work hours summary
- **Who's Out Today** - Colleagues on leave today
- **Upcoming Time Off** - Scheduled absences
- **Recently Approved** - Latest approved requests
- **Birthday Reminders** - Upcoming team celebrations
- **Hydration Tracker** - Water intake tracking (when enabled)

<Callout type="info">
Widget availability depends on your role and enabled features. Enable Hydration Tracker in **Settings** → **Wellness**.
</Callout>

## Navigation

Key sections you can access:
- **Dashboard** - Overview of your work and pending tasks
- **Time Tracking** - Log your work hours
- **Absences** - Request time off
- **Approvals** - Review team requests (if you're a manager)

## Managing Your Profile

### Personal Information

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click on your avatar in the top-right corner
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select **"Settings"** → **"Profile"**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Update your information:
    - **First Name** - Your given name
    - **Last Name** - Your family name
    - **Gender** - Optional (Male, Female, Other)
    - **Birthday** - Optional, used for birthday celebrations
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **"Save Changes"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Your name and email are visible to your managers and team members.
</Callout>

### Profile Picture

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click on your current avatar
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Upload a new image (recommended: square image, at least 200x200 pixels)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **"Save"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Email Notifications

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Scroll to **"Email Notifications"** section
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Toggle the notifications you want to receive:
    - Approval requests
    - Time entry reminders
    - Team announcements
    - Birthday notifications
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **"Save Changes"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Managers

### What are Managers?

Managers are employees who have approval authority for your:
- Vacation and time-off requests
- Time entry corrections
- Schedule changes

### Multiple Managers

You can have more than one manager! This is useful if you report to multiple people or work across teams.

**Primary Manager**: One of your managers is designated as "primary." They are:
- Your default approver for requests
- Listed first in your profile
- Your main point of contact for work-related matters

**Secondary Managers**: Additional managers who can also:
- Approve your requests
- View your work information
- Provide guidance and support

### Viewing Your Managers

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Scroll to the **"Managers"** section
  </Step>
  <Step>
    You'll see all your managers listed, with the primary manager clearly marked
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Only administrators can assign or change managers. If you need to update your managers, contact your admin.
</Callout>

## Team Membership

### Your Team

Teams help organize employees by department, project, or function. You may belong to one team.

### Viewing Your Team

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** → **Teams**
  </Step>
  <Step>

  </Step>
  <Step>

  </Step>
</Steps>

Being part of a team gives you:
- Shared vacation calendar visibility
- Team-specific announcements
- Easier collaboration with teammates
- Team-based reporting and analytics

## Work Schedules

### What is a Work Schedule?

Your work schedule defines your expected working hours. It's used to:
- Calculate vacation allowances
- Track time off accurately
- Ensure proper workload distribution

### Types of Work Schedules

#### Simple Schedule
- **Description**: One number representing total weekly hours
- **Example**: 40 hours per week
- **Best for**: Standard full-time or part-time schedules

#### Detailed Schedule
- **Description**: Specific hours for each day of the week
- **Example**: Monday-Friday 8 hours each, weekends off
- **Best for**: Irregular schedules or specific work patterns

### Viewing Your Schedule

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** → **Profile** or **Settings** → **Employees** → (Your Name)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Look for the **"Work Schedule"** section
  </Step>
  <Step>
    You'll see:
    - Total weekly hours
    - Work classification (daily, weekly, or monthly)
    - Effective date of the schedule
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Only administrators and managers can modify work schedules. If your schedule needs updating, contact your admin.
</Callout>

## Dark Mode & Themes

### Switching Themes

Z8 supports both light and dark themes for comfortable viewing:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click on your avatar in the top-right corner
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click the **theme toggle** button (sun/moon icon)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose your preference:
    - **Light**: Bright theme for well-lit environments
    - **Dark**: Dark theme for reduced eye strain
    - **System**: Automatically match your device settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Benefits of Dark Mode

- Reduced eye strain in low-light environments
- Lower battery consumption on OLED screens
- Better focus with less visual distraction
- Accessibility-friendly contrast options

## Overview

Z8 includes a wellness feature to help you stay hydrated throughout your workday. The hydration tracking system includes:

- **Dashboard Widget** - Track your daily water intake at a glance
- **Water Reminders** - Gentle notifications while you're clocked in
- **Streak Tracking** - Build healthy habits with gamification
- **Customizable Goals** - Set your own daily targets

---

## Dashboard Widget

The Hydration Tracker widget appears on your dashboard when wellness features are enabled:

### Features

- **Daily Progress** - See how many glasses you've had vs. your goal
- **Progress Bar** - Visual indicator of your daily target
- **Streak Counter** - Track consecutive days of meeting your goal
- **Quick Log Buttons** - Log 1 or 2 glasses with a single click

### Using the Widget

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Find the **Hydration Tracker** widget on your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **"+1 Glass"** or **"+2 Glasses"** to log water intake
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Watch your progress bar fill as you work toward your daily goal
  </Step>
  <Step>
    When you reach 100%, you'll see a celebration message
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The widget only appears when wellness features are enabled in your settings.
</Callout>

## Water Reminders

Get gentle reminders to drink water while you're clocked in at work.

### How It Works

- Reminders only appear **while you're clocked in**
- They stop when you clock out or take breaks
- Each reminder is a non-intrusive notification
- You can dismiss or snooze reminders

### Reminder Presets

| Preset | Interval | Best For |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Light** | Every 60 min | Low activity, sedentary work |
| **Moderate** | Every 45 min | Most office workers (recommended) |
| **Active** | Every 30 min | High activity, hot environments |
| **Custom** | 15-120 min | Your own preferred interval |

---

## Configuring Wellness Settings

### Enable Water Reminders

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** → **Wellness**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Toggle **"Enable Water Reminders"** on
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose a reminder preset:
    - Light (60 min)
    - Moderate (45 min) - Recommended
    - Active (30 min)
    - Custom (set your own interval)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **"Save Changes"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Set Your Daily Goal

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Settings** → **Wellness**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use the **Daily Goal** slider to set your target
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose between 1-20 glasses per day (default: 8)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **"Save Changes"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The recommended daily water intake is 8 glasses, but this varies based on activity level, climate, and personal health needs.
</Callout>

### Custom Reminder Interval

If the presets don't fit your needs:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Select **"Custom"** from the preset dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use the slider to set your interval (15-120 minutes)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **"Save Changes"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Streak Tracking

Build healthy habits by maintaining your hydration streak:

### How Streaks Work

- Meet your daily goal to continue your streak
- Missing a day resets your streak to 0
- Your longest streak is tracked for motivation
- Current streaks display on the dashboard widget

### Streak Display

When you have an active streak:
- An orange flame icon shows your current streak
- **"Personal best!"** appears when you match or beat your record
- The streak counter shows consecutive days of goal completion

## Tips for Staying Hydrated

1. **Start your day with water** - Drink a glass when you first clock in
2. **Keep water visible** - Place a water bottle at your desk
3. **Use reminder presets** - Start with "Moderate" and adjust as needed
4. **Log immediately** - Track drinks as you have them for accuracy
5. **Pair with breaks** - Drink water during your break reminders

<Callout type="info">
Proper hydration can improve focus, reduce fatigue, and boost overall well-being during your workday.
</Callout>

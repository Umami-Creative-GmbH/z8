## Requesting Time Off

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Absences** → **"Request Time Off"**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Fill in the form:
    - **Start Date** - When your time off begins
    - **End Date** - When you return to work
    - **Type** - Vacation, sick leave, personal day, etc.
    - **Reason** - Brief explanation (optional but recommended)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **"Submit Request"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Your manager(s) will be notified and can approve or deny the request.
</Callout>

### Requesting Time Off on Mobile

You can also manage your own absences in the mobile app:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **Absences** in the mobile app.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Tap **Request Absence**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter the category, start date, end date, day periods, and optional notes.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Submit the request and return to the list to track its status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Mobile Absence List">
  The mobile app groups your absences into **Upcoming**, **Pending**, and **Past** views. Pending requests can be cancelled directly from the list.
</Callout>

## Checking Request Status

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Absences**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    View the **"My Requests"** tab
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Check the status indicators:
    - **Pending** - Awaiting manager approval
    - **Approved** - Approved by your manager
    - **Denied** - Not approved (reason included)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Vacation Balance

Your vacation allowance is calculated based on:
- Organization policy (default days per year)
- Your work schedule (full-time vs part-time)
- Carryover from previous year
- Any manual adjustments (bonuses, etc.)

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Absences** → **"My Allowance"**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    You'll see:
    - Annual days granted
    - Days carried over
    - Adjustments (if any)
    - Days used this year
    - Remaining balance
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Approvals (For Managers)

<Callout type="info">
If you're a manager, you have additional responsibilities and permissions.
</Callout>

### Accessing Approvals

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Approvals** from the main navigation
  </Step>
  <Step>
    You'll see two tabs:
    - **Absence Requests** - Time-off requests
    - **Time Corrections** - Time entry corrections
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reviewing Requests

<Tabs items={['View Details', 'Approve', 'Deny']}>
  <Tab value="View Details">
    See dates, reason, and employee information before making a decision.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Approve">
    Grant the request - the employee will be notified immediately.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Deny">
    Reject with a reason - always provide an explanation to help the employee understand.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Best Practices for Managers

<Callout type="info" title="Manager Tips">
- **Review requests promptly** - Employees plan based on approvals
- **Be fair and consistent** - Apply the same standards to all team members
- **Provide reasons for denials** - Help employees understand decisions
- **Check team coverage** - Ensure adequate staffing before approving
- **Communicate** - Talk to employees about complex scheduling issues
</Callout>

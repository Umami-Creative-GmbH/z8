## General Questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my own manager?">
    No, only administrators can assign or change managers. Contact your admin if you need to update your managers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I have multiple managers and they give conflicting approvals?">
    Your primary manager's decision takes precedence. If there's a conflict, discuss with both managers to reach an agreement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see other employees' information?">
    You can see basic information (name, team) for your teammates. Detailed information (schedule, salary, personal data) is private and only visible to managers and administrators.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often is my vacation balance updated?">
    Your balance updates in real-time as requests are approved or denied. It resets annually based on your organization's policy.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Profile & Settings

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Is my birthday information required?">
    No, birthday is optional. It's used for birthday announcements and celebrations if you choose to share it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I hide my profile picture?">
    Profile pictures are visible to your organization. If you don't upload one, your initials will display instead.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What information is visible to my manager?">
    Your manager can see: name, email, position, team, work schedule, vacation balance, and request history. They cannot see personal notes or private information.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Work Schedule

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What if my schedule changes temporarily?">
    Contact your administrator to set up a new schedule with an effective date. The system tracks schedule history automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does my schedule affect my vacation days?">
    Yes! Part-time schedules may result in proportionally fewer vacation days. Your allowance is calculated based on your work schedule.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have different schedules for different weeks?">
    Currently, the system supports one active schedule at a time. For irregular schedules, use the detailed schedule option and work with your administrator for special cases.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Vacation & Absences

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel a request after submitting it?">
    Yes, if it's still pending. Contact your manager if it's already approved and you need to cancel.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to unused vacation days at year-end?">
    This depends on your organization's policy. Some organizations allow carryover, others have a "use it or lose it" policy. Check with your administrator.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I request time off for past dates?">
    Generally no, the system prevents backdating requests. If you need to record past absences, contact your manager or administrator.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How far in advance should I request time off?">
    Follow your organization's policy. Common practice is 2 weeks for vacation, but emergencies can be accommodated.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Technical Issues

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="I can't log in. What should I do?">
    1. Check your email and password are correct
    2. Try the "Forgot Password" link
    3. Clear your browser cache
    4. Contact your IT administrator if issues persist
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The page is loading slowly. How can I fix it?">
    1. Check your internet connection
    2. Try refreshing the page
    3. Close other browser tabs
    4. Try a different browser
    5. Contact IT support if problems continue
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I found a bug. How do I report it?">
    Contact your system administrator with:
    - Description of the problem
    - Steps to reproduce it
    - Screenshots if possible
    - Browser and device information
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Tips for Success

- **Keep your profile updated** - Accurate information helps everyone
- **Review your schedule regularly** - Make sure it reflects your current work pattern
- **Plan time off in advance** - Give your manager and team adequate notice
- **Check your vacation balance** - Avoid surprises at year-end
- **Communicate with your manager** - Open dialogue makes everything easier
- **Use the dashboard widgets** - Quick access to important information

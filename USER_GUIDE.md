## Getting Started

### First Time Login

When you first log in, you'll see:
- Your personal dashboard with relevant widgets
- Navigation menu to access different features
- Your profile information in the top-right corner

### Navigation

Key sections you can access:
- **Dashboard** - Overview of your work and pending tasks
- **Time Tracking** - Log your work hours
- **Absences** - Request time off
- **Approvals** - Review team requests (if you're a manager)
- **Settings** - Manage your profile and preferences

## Managing Your Profile

### Personal Information

To update your personal information:

1. Click on your avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select **"Settings"** → **"Profile"**
3. Update your information:
   - **First Name** - Your given name
   - **Last Name** - Your family name
   - **Gender** - Optional (Male, Female, Other)
   - **Birthday** - Optional, used for birthday celebrations
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

**Note**: Your name and email are visible to your managers and team members.

### Profile Picture

To change your profile picture:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Click on your current avatar
3. Upload a new image (recommended: square image, at least 200x200 pixels)
4. Click **"Save"**

## Understanding Managers

### What are Managers?

Managers are employees who have approval authority for your:
- Vacation and time-off requests
- Time entry corrections
- Schedule changes

### Multiple Managers

You can have more than one manager! This is useful if you report to multiple people or work across teams.

**Primary Manager**: One of your managers is designated as "primary." They are:
- Your default approver for requests
- Listed first in your profile
- Your main point of contact for work-related matters

**Secondary Managers**: Additional managers who can also:
- Approve your requests
- View your work information
- Provide guidance and support

### Viewing Your Managers

To see who your managers are:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Scroll to the **"Managers"** section
3. You'll see all your managers listed, with the primary manager clearly marked

**Note**: Only administrators can assign or change managers. If you need to update your managers, contact your admin.

## Work Schedules

### What is a Work Schedule?

Your work schedule defines your expected working hours. It's used to:
- Calculate vacation allowances
- Track time off accurately
- Ensure proper workload distribution

### Types of Work Schedules

#### Simple Schedule
- **Description**: One number representing total weekly hours
- **Example**: 40 hours per week
- **Best for**: Standard full-time or part-time schedules

#### Detailed Schedule
- **Description**: Specific hours for each day of the week
- **Example**: Monday-Friday 8 hours each, weekends off
- **Best for**: Irregular schedules or specific work patterns

### Viewing Your Schedule

To see your work schedule:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile** or **Settings** → **Employees** → (Your Name)
2. Look for the **"Work Schedule"** section
3. You'll see:
   - Total weekly hours
   - Work classification (daily, weekly, or monthly)
   - Effective date of the schedule

**Note**: Only administrators and managers can modify work schedules. If your schedule needs updating, contact your admin.

## Vacation & Time Off

### Requesting Time Off

To request vacation or time off:

1. Go to **Absences** → **"Request Time Off"**
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Start Date** - When your time off begins
   - **End Date** - When you return to work
   - **Type** - Vacation, sick leave, personal day, etc.
   - **Reason** - Brief explanation (optional but recommended)
3. Click **"Submit Request"**

Your manager(s) will be notified and can approve or deny the request.

### Checking Request Status

To see the status of your requests:
1. Go to **Absences**
2. View the **"My Requests"** tab
3. Status indicators:
   - **Pending** ⏳ - Awaiting manager approval
   - **Approved** ✅ - Approved by your manager
   - **Denied** ❌ - Not approved (reason included)

### Vacation Balance

Your vacation allowance is calculated based on:
- Organization policy (default days per year)
- Your work schedule (full-time vs part-time)
- Carryover from previous year
- Any manual adjustments (bonuses, etc.)

To view your balance:
1. Go to **Absences** → **"My Allowance"**
2. You'll see:
   - Annual days granted
   - Days carried over
   - Adjustments (if any)
   - Days used this year
   - Remaining balance

## Approvals (For Managers)

If you're a manager, you have additional responsibilities and permissions.

### Accessing Approvals

1. Go to **Approvals** from the main navigation
2. You'll see two tabs:
   - **Absence Requests** - Time-off requests
   - **Time Corrections** - Time entry corrections

### Reviewing Requests

For each pending request, you can:
- **View Details** - See dates, reason, and employee information
- **Approve** ✅ - Grant the request
- **Deny** ❌ - Reject with a reason
- **Request More Info** - Ask the employee for clarification

### Best Practices for Managers

✅ **Review requests promptly** - Employees plan based on approvals
✅ **Be fair and consistent** - Apply the same standards to all team members
✅ **Provide reasons for denials** - Help employees understand decisions
✅ **Check team coverage** - Ensure adequate staffing before approving
✅ **Communicate** - Talk to employees about complex scheduling issues

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Can I change my own manager?**
A: No, only administrators can assign or change managers. Contact your admin if you need to update your managers.

**Q: What if I have multiple managers and they give conflicting approvals?**
A: Your primary manager's decision takes precedence. If there's a conflict, discuss with both managers to reach an agreement.

**Q: Can I see other employees' information?**
A: You can see basic information (name, team) for your teammates. Detailed information (schedule, salary, personal data) is private and only visible to managers and administrators.

**Q: How often is my vacation balance updated?**
A: Your balance updates in real-time as requests are approved or denied. It resets annually based on your organization's policy.

### Profile & Settings

**Q: Is my birthday information required?**
A: No, birthday is optional. It's used for birthday announcements and celebrations if you choose to share it.

**Q: Can I hide my profile picture?**
A: Profile pictures are visible to your organization. If you don't upload one, your initials will display instead.

**Q: What information is visible to my manager?**
A: Your manager can see: name, email, position, team, work schedule, vacation balance, and request history. They cannot see personal notes or private information.

### Work Schedule

**Q: What if my schedule changes temporarily?**
A: Contact your administrator to set up a new schedule with an effective date. The system tracks schedule history automatically.

**Q: Does my schedule affect my vacation days?**
A: Yes! Part-time schedules may result in proportionally fewer vacation days. Your allowance is calculated based on your work schedule.

**Q: Can I have different schedules for different weeks?**
A: Currently, the system supports one active schedule at a time. For irregular schedules, use the detailed schedule option and work with your administrator for special cases.

### Vacation & Absences

**Q: Can I cancel a request after submitting it?**
A: Yes, if it's still pending. Contact your manager if it's already approved and you need to cancel.

**Q: What happens to unused vacation days at year-end?**
A: This depends on your organization's policy. Some organizations allow carryover, others have a "use it or lose it" policy. Check with your administrator.

**Q: Can I request time off for past dates?**
A: Generally no, the system prevents backdating requests. If you need to record past absences, contact your manager or administrator.

**Q: How far in advance should I request time off?**
A: Follow your organization's policy. Common practice is 2 weeks for vacation, but emergencies can be accommodated.

### Technical Issues

**Q: I can't log in. What should I do?**
A:
1. Check your email and password are correct
2. Try the "Forgot Password" link
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Contact your IT administrator if issues persist

**Q: The page is loading slowly. How can I fix it?**
A:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Close other browser tabs
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact IT support if problems continue

**Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?**
A: Contact your system administrator with:
- Description of the problem
- Steps to reproduce it
- Screenshots if possible
- Browser and device information
